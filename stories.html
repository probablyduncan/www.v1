About

Hi! I'm Duncan Petrie. I grew up in Milwaukee, Wisconsin, but I'm currently in Falmouth, Cornwall, studying Marine & Natural History Photography. I've been writing all my life and photographing plants and birds and rocks and things since 2016. In the autumn of 2019, I taught myself HTML, CSS, and Javascript to build this website. Look here for a look into my photographic process, here for my artist statement, or here for a good story.

For prints or business inqueries, or if you're one capable dude short of an adventure, email me at duncanpetrie1@gmail.com, or message me on Instagram @probablyduncan.

And if I don't see you, good afternoon, good evening, and goodnight.
