Iceland


2018-2019


Arctic foxes roam the high fjords of Hornstrandir.

The midnight sun paints reaching clouds across the wet sands of Breiðavík.

Clouds drift over stark ridges just south of Borgarnes.

As the sun sets over Sólheimasandur, rays of light pierce the heavy storm clouds.

Cold glacier melt shoots down the mountain in Skaftafell.

The loose glass and chipped frames of Stöng guard against sporadic highland weather.

A ram and its lamb attempt to hitchhike, despite a clear thumb inadequacy.

Two lambs trot across the gravel road that connects the quiet Jökulsárgljúfur valley with tourist-thronged Detifoss.

A rhyolite canyon scars Borgarfjarðarvegur.

Part of Hvannadalshnúkur, the tallest peak in Iceland, rises above the boreal landscape.

The tortuous rhyolite canyon Innra Hvannagil twists up into the mist.

Just west of Vik, the sun hesitates behind a mountain.

A lonely hiker treks across Sólheimasandur.
