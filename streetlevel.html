Street Level


Photographed / Written
Summer 2018

A bus flies down Amsterdam Avenue after a hard rain.



In a rare moment of serenity, a New Yorker pauses his commute, waiting for traffic.

Many feet cross puddles left by a recent rainstorm. On the road, taxi drivers, commuters, and police vie for dominance.

The sun, doubly reflected off of two Lincoln Center windows, shines through rainclouds.

A pedestrian crosses 62nd street.

Two men pass the time.
